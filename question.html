Will all of the items in my order arrive in a single package?

Your items will be delivered separately if your order includes items from multiple sellers. Items from a single seller that are part of your order may or may not ship together. You may be confident that the remaining item(s) will be delivered soon even if your orders arrive in separate deliveries.

Who will deliver my order?

Your order may be delivered by KOS, by other courier companies, or by the seller or the seller's agents, depending on whether the item was sold by Asap or a third-party seller and on the delivery method chosen by a third-party seller. You may be called prior to or on the same day as delivery to see if you are available to receive your order.

Why does the estimated delivery time vary?

Ausmobile is a true online marketplace that empowers Nigerians across the country. This means that not all products on Asap are sold or delivered by Asap. In addition, Ausmobile buyers and sellers are found in every state in Nigeria. Given the various factors involved (the time it takes a seller to ship, the courier company used, the distance between buyer and seller, and more) delivery times may vary.
